[h1] Bot Tweaks [/h1]

This mod allows the player to improve the behavior of all of the miscellaneous robots in the game. These include Rover, Biobot, Sweepy, Flydo, and Remote Worker. 

[h1] Features [/h1]

* Adds ability to remove Power Banks from a robot once loaded
* Adds ability to Deconstruct Flydo
* Adds ability to Deconstruct Rover and Biobot at any time, not just when they are shut down
* Prevents multiple Atomic Power Banks from being loaded into a robot
* Ensures the Atomic Power Bank is utilized before other power banks when multiple are loaded in in a robot
* (Optional) Add Power Banks to Rover and Biobot to continue using them after shut down
* (Optional) Automatically mark Rover for destruction when shut down
* (Optional) Avoid automatically marking Biobot for destruction when shut down
* View and track all robots on the Resources Screen and Pinned Resources Panel
